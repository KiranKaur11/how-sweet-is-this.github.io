Discover the World of M&M's

Welcome to the colorful world of M&M's! Discover the joy that these little candies bring.

Fun Facts

  • M&M's were created in 1941 by Forrest Mars and Bruce Murrie.
  • The candy coating on M&M's was developed to prevent melting in your hands.
  • There are currently more than 30 different colors of M&M's.
  • M&M’s are colorful little sugar-coated chocolate pieces that have stolen the hearts of generations.

Nutritional Information

  • A Healthier Snack than You Think!
  • Serving Size: 1 pack (45g)
  • Calories: 220
  • They’re low in cholesterol and sodium, which can easily drive up your weight when consumed in excess.
